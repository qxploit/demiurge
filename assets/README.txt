GAME ASSETS PACK
================
A bundle of 16x16 pixel-art asset packs by Shade (merchant-shade) and
octoshrimpy. Most are CC0 (public domain): free for commercial and
non-commercial use, modification and redistribution, no credit required.
EXCEPTION: RPGIcons_CC-BY-SA/ is CC-BY-SA 4.0 (attribution + share-alike) --
kept in its own folder; treat separately from the CC0 packs.

CONTENTS
--------
MiniWorldSprites/   100+ characters, 135+ buildings (team-colored), terrain,
                    nature, animals, objects, UI.
                    itch: https://merchant-shade.itch.io/16x16-mini-world-sprites
                    OGA:  https://opengameart.org/content/miniworld-sprites

PunyCharacters/     16x16 base character sprites + editor edition + orcs.
                    itch: https://merchant-shade.itch.io/16x16-puny-characters-plus-sprites
                    OGA:  https://opengameart.org/content/puny-characters

PunyWorld/          16x16 overworld tileset (single sheet PNG).
                    itch: https://merchant-shade.itch.io/16x16-puny-world
                    OGA:  https://opengameart.org/content/16x16-puny-world-tileset

PunyDungeon/        16x16 dungeon tileset.
                    itch: https://merchant-shade.itch.io/16x16-puny-dungeon
                    OGA:  https://opengameart.org/content/16x16-puny-dungeon-tileset

MiniWorldPlus/      octoshrimpy's expansion/rework of MiniWorldSprites, as large
                    atlas sheets (buildings, terrain, nature, walls, indoor_things,
                    alives_orcs, alives_other, chests, things, terrain_decor).
                    Two variants: indoor-update and rainbow-refresh.
                    https://octoshrimpy.itch.io/miniworld-plus

RPGIcons_CC-BY-SA/  20+ 16x16 RPG icons (weapons/potions/food/gear) + recolors.
                    ** CC-BY-SA 4.0, NOT CC0 ** -- credit Shade, share-alike applies.
                    itch: https://merchant-shade.itch.io/16x16-mixed-rpg-icons
                    OGA:  https://opengameart.org/content/16x16-rpg-icons
